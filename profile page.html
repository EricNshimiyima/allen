WHO WE ARE

Ecole Secondaire Saint Joseph le Travailleur (ESSJT) is a private Catholic school owned by

the Archdiocese of Kigali. It was founded in 1985 and accredited in accordance

with convention no 77/08.00/06 on April 14th, 1993 signed between the Ministry

of Secondary and Primary School (MINEPRISEC) and Archdiocese of Kigali. It is

located in Kigali downtown, precisely in Ubumwe Cell, Muhima Sector, Nyarugenge District, in the City of Kigali.

ESSJT follows Rwanda National Curriculum.
